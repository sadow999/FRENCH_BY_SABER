Chapitre 1: Les Premiers Mots

The art of conversation begins with a simple hello. Let's make it sound perfect.

Listen to a conversation with these words:

Bonjour! / Bonsoir!

بون-چووور / بون-سواار

Bonjour (Hello): The "on" is nasal (غُنّة). The "j" is soft (چ) and the "r" is from the throat. Use it all day.

Bonsoir (Good evening): Use it when evening comes.

Je m'appelle...

النطق: چوه ما-پيل...

المعنى: أنا اسمي...

Enchanté / Enchantée

النطق: أون-شون-تيه

المعنى: Delighted to meet you. (Enchanté for male, Enchantée for female, same sound).

Listen to asking "How are you?":

Comment allez-vous? (Formal) / Ça va? (Informal)

كو-مون تا-ليه ڤو؟ / صا ڤا؟

Pro Tip (Liaison): In "Comment allez-vous", the silent "t" links to the "a", creating the fluent "ta-lay" sound. This is a key to sounding French!

الردود (Replies): Très bien (Very well), Bien (Well), Ça va (Okay), Pas mal (Not bad), Bof... (Meh...).

Chapitre 2: La Boîte à Outils (The Survival Toolkit)

Your essential phrases to ask questions and navigate any conversation.

Listen to these phrases in action:

Excusez-moi, comment dit-on «...» en français?

إكس-كوزيه موا، كو-مون دي-تون «...» أون فرون-سيه؟

المعنى: "Excuse me, how do you say '...' in French?"

Ça s'écrit comment?

صا سيه-كري كو-مون؟

المعنى: "How is that written?" (Perfect for getting the spelling).

Vous pouvez répéter, s'il vous plaît?

ڤو پو-ڤيه ريه-پيه-تيه، سيل ڤو پليه؟

المعنى: "Could you repeat that, please?" (Say it with a musical rhythm!).

Je ne sais pas.

چوه نوه سيه پا.

المعنى: "I don't know." (The `ne...pas` creates the negative).

The Rules of 'C' and 'G'

حرف C:
• ينطق "ك" (K sound) قبل A, O, U. مثال: cocacola, calculatrice, cube.
• ينطق "س" (S sound) قبل E, I. مثال: merci, cinema.
• الحالة الخاصة (Ç): The "cedilla" (ç) always makes it an "S" sound, no matter what. مثال: français, garçon.

حرف G:
• ينطق "ج" مصرية (Hard G) قبل A, O, U. مثال: garçon, gomme.
• ينطق "چ" معطشة (Soft J) قبل E, I. مثال: girafe, gilet.

Les Accents & Sound Groups

العلامات (Accents):
• é (aigu): Makes a short, sharp "ay" sound. مثال: répétér.
• è, à (grave): Makes a longer, open "eh" sound. مثال: mère.
• ê, â, ô (circonflexe): Lengthens the vowel sound.
• ë (tréma): Pronounce two vowels separately. مثال: Noël (no-el).

مجموعات الحروف (Sound Groups):
• "ou" sound: o / eau / au (all sound like 'o' in "go"). مثال: chapeau, tableau.
• Nasal "an/on" sound: in / im / un / ain / ien. مثال: lundi, pain, égyptien.
• "ay" sound: ai / ais / er / ez / et / ait (all sound like 'ay' in "say"). مثال: j'ai, parler, nez.

Asking for the Date

للسؤال عن اليوم: Quel jour sommes-nous? (رسمي) / C'est quel jour? (غير رسمي)

الإجابة: Nous sommes le samedi. (Today is Saturday.)

الإجابة: Nous sommes le 26 juillet. (It's July 26th.)

Chapitre 5: Dans la Salle de Classe

Understanding objects and the golden rule of French: Masculine & Feminine.

Le/La & Un/Une: The Golden Rule

In French, every noun has a gender, even objects. You must learn the gender with the word!

أدوات التعريف (The):
• le: for masculine words (le garçon, le crayon)
• la: for feminine words (la chaise, la fenêtre)

أدوات النكرة (A/An):
• un: for masculine words (un garçon, un crayon)
• une: for feminine words (une chaise, une fenêtre)
